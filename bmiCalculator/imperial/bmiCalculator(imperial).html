How to use the calculator

Simply click on the measuring system you use (imperial or metric) and enter in your weight and height. Finally, get your BMI, BMI status, and advice for it!

How the calculator works

This calculator works by taking in your weight and height. Then it applies the formula (weight(kg)/(height(cm)^2)) for metric and (703*weight(lb.)/(height(in)^2)) for imperial. Then it classifies the BMI into 4 categories (Underweight, Healthy, Overweight, and Obese). Underweight is a BMI under 18.5. Healthy is a BMI from 18.5-24.9. Overweight is considered a BMI from 25-29.9. Obese is a BMI over 30.
