How to use the calculator

Simply click on the measuring system you use (imperial or metric), and enter your weight, height, age, mother’s height, and father’s height. You can now see your future height estimates and how to increase your height as a child!

How the calculator works

This calculator works by taking in your weight, height, and gender. Then it finds your BMI. For metric, the formula is: (weight(kg)/(height(cm)^2)). For imperial, the formula is: (703*weight(lb.)/(height(in)^2). Then it uses a formula to find your body fat percentage as follows. (1.39*bmi) + (0.16 * age) - (10.34 * 1)-20. Then it classifies the body fat percentage into various categories. Obese is considered above 25 percent. Healthy is considered 6-25 percent. Underweight is considered under 6 percent.
