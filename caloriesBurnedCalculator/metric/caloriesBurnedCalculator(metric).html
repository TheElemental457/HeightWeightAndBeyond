How to use the calculator

Simply click on the measuring system you use (imperial or metric), and enter your weight, height, age, gender, activity level, and target weight loss/gain. Now you can see how many calories you should consume a day!

How the calculator works

This calculator works by taking in your weight, duration, and intensity level based on MET values of activities. If you are using the imperial system, behind the scenes its converts weight into metric. Then it uses the following formula: duration*intensity*3.5*weight/200.
