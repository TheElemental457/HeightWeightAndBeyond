Our purpose

Due to the pandemic, a large part of the global population is overweight or obese because of lack of physical activity. This increases the threat of heart disease and illnesses such as diabetes. Along with this, some children are not growing to their expected height because of a sedentary lifestyle and erratic food habits. Our goal with this app is to help people become aware of their growth potential and help improve their health outcomes. If our app helps even one person, it will all be worth it!

Our vision

Easy to use health awareness apps can be made to provide people insights about their health to make better choices when it comes to excercise and diet.
