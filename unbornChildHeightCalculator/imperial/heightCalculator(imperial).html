How to use the calculator

Simply click on the measuring system you use (imperial or metric) and enter your height and your spouse’s height to see how tall your children can be!

How the calculator works

This calculator works by taking the mom’s height and the dad’s height. If you are using the metric system, behind the scenes it converts weight and height into imperial. Then it finds the average of the heights and adds 2.5 or subtracts 2.5 depending on if the child is a boy or girl. So, the formula goes as follows. Boy: (Mom’s height + Dad’s height)/2+2.5. Girl: (Mom’s height + Dad's height)/2-2.5. (Note that this calculator uses a quite simple formula, and you can expect a difference of 4 inches or 10.15 centimeters).
